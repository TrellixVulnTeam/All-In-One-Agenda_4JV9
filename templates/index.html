About

The All-In-One Agenda is a program built to simplify and ease your life. This website is a scheduling/calendar program that lets you create tasks, add events, set alarms, schedule events, and plan your life out. Our goal was to simplify your life, and what better way to simplify your life than to have a simple, easy-to-use web application. Our application's goal was to be simplify life and fundamentally be simple for everyone.
